Frequently Asked Questions

Can I listen to music on the website?

Sort of. We are in the process of moving our files to mega.nz - a malware-free location where you can download AND listen to mp3 files. Navigate to the home page, select your desired artist, choose a compilation, and you'll be redirected to the site where you can listen.

Can I listen to music on my phone?

Yes. Refer to the previous question and follow the same instructions. After selecting an artist, choose a compilation, and a new window will pop up at mega.nz where you can listen to the music.

How do I download?

Navigate to the home page and select the photo of an artist. You will be redirected to all of our available compilations for that artist. Select the download button of your desired compilation. You'll be redirected to mega.nz where you can download a .zip file malware free. Download the .zip file ,unzip the files and rage away.

How do I move the mp3 files onto my phone?

Unzip your .zip file by double clicking it. A regular folder should appear. Drag and drop that folder into a music streaming source that is connected to your cellular device (iTunes, Spotify, Play Music). The album info/artwork are pre-configured. Sync your phone and you're gucci.
