Why is this not working on my phone?

You're currently viewing trapple music's 1.0 design which, unfortunately, does not support mobile. You can only view photos. Get on a computer and check us out though! 2.0 will release in 2020.

Can I listen to the music on this website?

Yes. We are in the process of moving our files to mega.nz - a malware-free location where you can download AND listen to mp3 files. Select your desired artist, choose a compilation, and you'll be redirected to the site where you can listen.

How to download?

Select the photo of an artist. The popup should contain our available compilations with descriptions. Select the title of your desired compilation. You'll be redirected to Google Drive or Mega.nz where you can download a .zip file. Download the .zip file ,unzip the files and rage away.

How to move your mp3 files onto your phone?

Unzip your .zip file by double clicking it. A regular folder should appear. Drag and drop that folder into a music streaming source that is connected to your cellular device (iTunes, Spotify, Play Music). The album info/artwork are pre-configured. Sync your phone and you're gucci.
